Сore product - PogrAI

Our core product PogrAI is a platform that enables teams and individual developers to easily create AI-powered games and educational applications.

The Problem and the Solution

Game and app development is an exciting but complex process that requires technical skills, time, and significant resources. Many developers face barriers that limit their potential. But what if there was a solution that made game and app creation easier and accessible to everyone? This is exactly what PogrAI offers.

Programming Complexity

Creating games requires knowledge of programming languages, frameworks and game engines. A powerful AI will create the entire UI, game world, characters, game mechanics, and all game logic for you, allowing you to focus on creativity rather than technical aspects.

Creativity Limitations

Technical difficulties force developers to simplify their ideas and restrict possibilities. A flexible game creation system gives developers full creative freedom. A vast collection of templates and built-in assets not only removes limitations but also inspires and fuels creativity.

Multi-Platform Support

Developing a game for multiple platforms (desktop, mobile, web) requires significant effort and time. Since our application is available on all these platforms, games and applications created within it are automatically supported across all of them without extra work.

Long Development Cycles

Developing a game can take months or even years, requiring significant effort and resources. A powerful AI, along with a vast collection of templates and assets, drastically reduces development time.

High Resources Costs

High-quality graphics, animations, sounds, and other assets require significant investment. Extensive asset libraries and built-in resources generation tools reduce costs and accelerate the process.

AI Integration Challenges

Implementing AI in games requires specialized knowledge and experience. Simple AI integration through the platform makes it accessible even without deep expertise.

PogrAI removes the barriers that hinder game development, allowing anyone to bring their ideas to life without complexity or high costs. The future of game creation is accessible and innovative — and it starts here.

PogrAI

PogrAI is an innovative platform that simplifies the entire game and app creation process. Using a unified declarative model, developers can leverage any modern AI model to generate and validate their projects effortlessly. The platform supports mobile, desktop, and web, with AI-driven localization for a global audience. A built-in marketplace allows instant publishing and access to resources like graphics, animations, and sounds. AI is deeply integrated, enabling intelligent characters, interactive experiences, and multiplayer functionality. High-quality vector graphics ensure stunning visuals on any screen. With PogrAI, development becomes faster, easier, and more accessible than ever.

From Idea to Implementation

A unified, standardized, declarative model for describing a game or application makes it easy to use any modern large language model (LLM) for generation. This provides greater freedom in choosing between open-source or commercial LLMs. The built-in engine allows you to both run the game or application and validate the correctness of the generated app model. A vast collection of games and applications templates, along with ready-made prompts, makes implementation effortless - eliminating the need to start from scratch and ensuring that you can seamlessly request changes from the LLM without worrying about formulation.

Multi-platform and localization

We strive to make PogrAI accessible to as many people as possible. That’s why our application supports major mobile and desktop platforms, as well as the web. This means you can create and play games anywhere, on any device. For developers, this is a huge advantage, as their games or apps can reach the widest possible audience. And with the power of AI, we provide accurate localization into multiple languages, opening the door to the global market.

Marketplace

With PogrAI, publishing your game or application becomes as simple as possible — just one click, and it's available to the entire community. Our shared marketplace, with a classification and rating system, helps users easily find exciting projects. Additionally, the marketplace offers a variety of resources for game and app creation, including graphics, animations, sounds, music, and much more. This not only simplifies the development process but also fosters collaboration within the community.

AI Inside

In PogrAI, AI plays a key role not only in creating games and applications but also within them. Imagine interacting with AI-driven characters who act as opponents, companions, assistants, or teachers. This unlocks endless possibilities for diversity and depth in both gameplay and educational experiences. Additionally, we support a multiplayer mode, enabling interaction with friends or other users worldwide. This enhances the social aspect, making games and learning even more engaging.

High quality graphics

Support for vector graphics and animation ensures that images always remain crisp and beautiful, regardless of resolution or screen size. At the same time, vector formats take up less space compared to raster images. To achieve this, we use SVG and Lottie formats, which are supported in Flutter. This allows developers to easily integrate high-quality graphics and animations into their games and applications.

Market, Target Audience, and Monetization

Market Size and Prospects

The modern markets of AI development, gaming, and EdTech are experiencing rapid growth, creating unique opportunities for projects that combine these fields.

  • AI Development: According to Grand View Research, the global large language model powered tools market size was estimated at USD 1.43 billion in 2023 and is projected to grow at a CAGR of 48.8% from 2024 to 2030.
  • Gaming: Statista reports that the Video Games market in the second biggest market within Digital Media. The worldwide revenue of US$384.9 billion in 2023 is expected to grow to US$521.6 billion in 2027.
  • EdTech: According to market.us, the Global EdTech Market size is expected to be worth around USD 810.3 Billion by 2033, from USD 220.5 Billion in 2023, growing at a CAGR of 13.9% during the forecast period from 2024 to 2033.

Conclusion: The intersection of AI, gaming, and EdTech represents a high-potential niche where PogrAI can establish leadership by leveraging the synergy of three booming industries.

Target Audience

PogrAI platform is focused on meeting the needs of gamers across various genres and game developers, offering the opportunity to simplify complex programming processes, reduce development cycles, and expand creative horizons for the creation of innovative game projects.

Additionally, the platform enables the creation of educational applications, which will be beneficial for education startups, schools, teachers, publishers of educational courses, and students.

Business Model and Monetization

The PogrAI platform follows a freemium business model, offering basic functionality for free so that users can explore the potential of creating games and educational applications with AI. In the initial phase, monetization is implemented through unobtrusive advertising in the free version, establishing a steady revenue stream without putting undue pressure on users. Next, a donation system will be introduced, allowing users to voluntarily support the platform's development. Later, an option to purchase the full version of the application will be available, granting access to all features without ads or limitations, followed by a subscription service that increases daily content generation limits and unlocks premium features such as advanced creation tools, additional slots for games and educational apps, and enhanced AI for resource generation. Additionally, plans include developing a marketplace for selling paid games, educational applications, and resources, as well as securing grants and investments for further growth, and launching various merchandise to create an extra revenue stream.

PogrAI Development Roadmap

Version 1.0.0 – Basic Functionality and First Publication

At this stage, the universal game model is fully defined and a technology for implementing the engine core is selected. Minimal capabilities are implemented to play a basic game template. The application is published on Google Play Market, with all resources manually added to the package, and a built-in tic-tac-toe game model is created manually.

Version 1.0.1 – Model Integration and Account Support

This phase involves selecting the best-performing open model and deploying it on one of the providers. Integration with the model’s API is implemented, along with the creation of a chat screen for interacting with the model and viewing changes in the game model (initially in JSON format). Basic screens for login, logout, account settings, registration, and password recovery are developed, and authentication support is implemented. The application is also published on Microsoft Store.

Version 1.0.2 – Multiplayer and Web Presence

Multiplayer game mode is introduced, enabling online play. In parallel, the server-side logic for a future store of published games and resources is developed. A website (pograi.com) is launched featuring a web version of the platform and essential additional pages.

Version 1.0.3 – Interface Expansion and Marketplace

The platform receives a comprehensive main game selection window and an integrated marketplace screen, where placeholders displaying "In Development" replace game banners. The application is published on the App Store. We are creating a roadmap for games spanning many future versions, outlining the logic that should be implemented in the GameEngine and AIGeneration, and grouping games based on this logic.

Version 1.0.4 – Resource Base Expansion

At this stage, the application is published on the Mac App Store, and a screen for adding and managing the resource library is introduced, ensuring convenient content management and updates.

Version 1.0.5 – Linux Support and AI Game Generation

The application is released on Flathub (Linux) and integrates the capability to generate several games using AI based on the tic-tac-toe template, showcasing the potential of automated content generation.

Monetization Integration (Subsequent Versions)

In parallel with further functional development, monetization elements are gradually integrated:

  • Implementation of platform-specific purchase screens for the extended version of the application.
  • Introduction of logic to support user-published games and the purchase of our paid games.
  • Gradual rollout of additional paid features, such as premium accounts, subscriptions for increased content generation, and exclusive resources.

Further Development

In subsequent versions, new functional capabilities will be implemented according to the overall roadmap:

  • Development of help screens, feedback screens, a universal multi-level game model preview widget, and support for localizations.
  • Expansion of the game engine’s functionality and improvement of AI: supporting various 2D game mechanics and genres, with regular updates to generation algorithms.
  • Introduction of an expert mode in the editor for advanced users to fine-tune every aspect of the game universe.
  • Development of an AI-based resource generator capable of producing graphics, animations, sounds, music, and other elements.
  • Implementation of voice control to enable more intuitive platform interaction.
  • Deployment of an interactive AI-powered assistance system that serves as a personal guide for users throughout the development process.
